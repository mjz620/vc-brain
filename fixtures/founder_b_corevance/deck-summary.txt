--- SLIDE 1 · Company ---
Corevance is an AI copilot that drafts and maintains SOC 2 / ISO 27001 compliance
evidence for mid-market software companies. Connect your cloud infra; Corevance
drafts the evidence artifacts and flags drift between your controls and reality.

--- SLIDE 2 · Founder ---
Devin Marsh, Founder & CEO.
Previously: Infrastructure Engineer at Stripe (2017–2020).
Prior startup: DataLoom (data-pipeline tooling). Founded March 2019; served as
full-time CEO. DataLoom raised a $4M seed and was acquired by Cisco in 2021.

--- SLIDE 3 · Problem ---
Mid-market companies spend hundreds of hours per year assembling audit evidence.
The work is manual, repetitive, and error-prone; controls drift out of compliance
between audits and nobody notices until the next one.

--- SLIDE 4 · Product ---
Corevance connects to cloud infrastructure, drafts evidence artifacts automatically,
and continuously flags drift between documented controls and observed reality.
Live today. Design partners: Northwind Systems, Halcyon Labs.
