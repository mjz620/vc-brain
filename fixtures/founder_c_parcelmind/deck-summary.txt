PARCELMIND — Seed Deck (summary slides)
Applicants: Priya Anand, Marcus Ohl · Contact: founders@parcelmind.example · Submitted: 2026-07-18
Raising: $1,200,000 seed · Claimed runway: 18 months

--- SLIDE 1 · Company ---
Parcelmind automates freight-quote comparison and carrier negotiation for mid-size
shippers. Equivalent quotes across carriers vary severalfold, and procurement today
is manual and slow. We ingest shipment specs, collect quotes, and negotiate within
guardrails the shipper sets.

--- SLIDE 2 · Founders ---
Priya Anand — 5 years in freight procurement at a TMS vendor / FreightWaves ecosystem.
Marcus Ohl — 4 years building carrier-rate systems; named inventor on a dynamic
carrier rate-negotiation patent.
Combined ~9 years in freight and TMS.

--- SLIDE 3 · Problem ---
Mid-size shippers overpay because comparing carrier quotes is manual, and they lack
the leverage and time to negotiate. Enterprise shippers have TMS teams; mid-market
does not.

--- SLIDE 4 · Product ---
Live today. API integrations with two major TMS platforms: MercuryGate and Blue
Yonder TMS. Parcelmind ingests specs, collects quotes, and negotiates within
shipper-set guardrails.
