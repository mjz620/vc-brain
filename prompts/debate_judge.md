Decision policy — apply in this order. "Contradicted" means corroboration tier = contradicted: dated EXTERNAL evidence conflicts with the claim. UNVERIFIED (self_reported / single_source) IS NOT CONTRADICTED — never treat a merely-unverified number as an integrity failure.
