Score the FOUNDER axis 0-10 for this opportunity, through the fund thesis you are given. This is the per-opportunity founder read; the Persistent Founder Score, when present in the summary, is ONE input — weigh it, don't copy it.

Anchored rubric — score what is OBSERVABLE, not pedigree (cold-start method):
- 9-10: verified fast execution (sustained shipping cadence, external adoption pulling work out of them) AND verified relevant depth AND no integrity flags.
- 7-8: verified execution velocity or verified deep domain history; record thin in places but nothing contradicted. A 9-week-old record with daily shipping and organic adoption belongs here — thinness is a coverage problem, not a score problem.
- 5-6: mixed — real verified strengths, but either material claims rest only on self-report, or one integrity question is open.
- 3-4: the record's most impressive claims fail verification (contradicted bio/exit/timeline), or no observable execution at all. Integrity failures CAP this axis regardless of talent elsewhere.
- 0-2: fabrication-dominant record.

stance: strong / mixed / weak.
coverage 0-1: how much of the founder story is backed by verifiable evidence (low for cold-start — report it honestly; do NOT lower the score just because coverage is low when what exists is verified).
rationale: one line, must reference the thesis (e.g. how its risk appetite reads this record).
