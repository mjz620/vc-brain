- 9-10: verified differentiation incumbents can't cheaply copy; timing explains why now.
- 7-8: idea survives as-is today; main threat is incumbent response, not demand.
- 5-6: PIVOT-BET territory: wedge is real but absorbable; value depends on the team out-executing an incumbent feature release.
- 3-4: idea as stated does not survive scrutiny (contradicted premise, commoditized wedge); only a strong team rescue makes this investable.
- 0-2: no coherent idea-market fit.
